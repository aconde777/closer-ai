AI Scoring

Know exactly what to fix after every call.

An AI coach breaks down your performance across the four categories that actually determine whether you close or lose in high-ticket sales.

Objection Handling
Did you address the objection with confidence and a clear reframe, or did you fold? This is the difference between closers and order-takers.
Tonality
Were you calm, certain, and in control? Or did you sound desperate, needy, or unsure? Tone closes more deals than the words you say.
Closing Attempt
Did you actually ask for the commitment? Most reps talk themselves out of deals they already won by never pulling the trigger.
Rapport
Did you build a real connection or go straight to pitch mode? In high-ticket sales, people buy from people they trust -- not the best pitch.
